Bouldering in Castilla La Mancha

Castilla-La Mancha sits on the high central plateau of Spain, where bouldering happens on sandstone, quartzite, and granite blocks across surprisingly varied terrain. The continental climate means scorching summers and cold winters, so the sweet spots are autumn and spring when the high-altitude areas offer perfect friction temperatures. The style varies by rock type but tends toward technical faces and crimpy problems, with some excellent sit starts on lower blocks. This is an uncrowded bouldering region where you are likely to have sectors entirely to yourself.
