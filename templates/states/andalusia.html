Bouldering in Andalusia

Bouldering in Andalusia thrives on a mix of limestone, sandstone, and granite scattered across mountain ranges from Cadiz to Jaen. The style leans toward technical face problems and crimpy overhangs, with the occasional friction slab on granite. Thanks to the mild Mediterranean climate, the best season runs from October through April, though higher-altitude spots stay cool enough for summer sessions. Andalusia is also one of the few regions in Spain where you can boulder near the coast and in the mountains on the same trip.
