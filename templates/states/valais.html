Bouldering in Valais

Valais bouldering benefits from one of the driest climates in Switzerland, with abundant sunshine that keeps conditions dry even when other Alpine regions are rained out. The canton features granite and gneiss boulders scattered across high-altitude valleys, offering a spectrum of styles from friction-dependent slabs to steep, powerful overhangs. The best season runs from March through November, with the higher-altitude sectors providing cool temperatures for summer sessions that would be impossible at lower elevations.
