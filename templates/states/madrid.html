Bouldering in Madrid

Madrid's bouldering revolves around La Pedriza, a vast granite boulder field inside the Sierra de Guadarrama National Park, just an hour from the capital. The coarse-grained granite rewards precise footwork on slabs and friction-dependent faces, with problems ranging from gentle introductions to some of Spain's hardest highballs. The best conditions come from October through April when cool, dry air maximizes skin friction on the crystalline rock. Having one of Europe's largest bouldering areas so close to a major capital city makes Madrid uniquely accessible -- you can combine world-class sessions with big-city culture effortlessly.
