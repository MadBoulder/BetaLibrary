    Bouldering in Annot

    Annot, nestled in the stunning French Alps, is a premier bouldering destination renowned for its pristine sandstone boulders. Climbers flock to this area to tackle its varied and challenging problems, which include a mix of slopers, crimps, and dynamic movements. The forested landscape adds an enchanting atmosphere to the climbing experience, making it a favorite among both locals and international visitors. Annot offers a wide range of routes suitable for climbers of all skill levels, with grades from easy circuits to more demanding problems up to 8B+.

    The best time to visit Annot is during the cooler months, typically from fall to spring, when the weather conditions are ideal for climbing. This period ensures excellent friction on the sandstone, enhancing the climbing experience. The area's magical blend of nature and rock, combined with its moderate climate and easy access, has contributed to its growing popularity. The picturesque setting, combined with world-class bouldering opportunities, makes Annot a must-visit destination for climbing enthusiasts seeking both challenge and beauty in the French Alps​.
