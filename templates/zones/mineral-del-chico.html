    Bouldering in Mineral del Chico

    Mineral del Chico, located in the state of Hidalgo, Mexico, is a renowned bouldering destination that attracts climbers from all over due to its beautiful granite rock formations. The area is part of the Parque Nacional El Chico, which offers a stunning natural backdrop of forested landscapes and scenic vistas, enhancing the climbing experience.

    The bouldering in Mineral del Chico features a variety of problems ranging from easy to highly challenging, making it suitable for climbers of all skill levels. The granite rocks provide excellent friction, with a mix of cracks, slabs, and overhangs that test both technique and strength. The area is known for its good weather conditions, allowing for climbing throughout most of the year.
