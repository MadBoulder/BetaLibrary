    Bouldering in Bagni di Masino

    Bagni di Masino, located in Italy, is a hidden gem for bouldering enthusiasts, known for its stunning granite boulders and challenging rock formations. Situated in the Val Masino valley, this area offers a unique climbing experience in an unparalleled natural setting. Popular sectors like Belvedere and ERSAF provide climbers with a variety of boulder problems, from technical slabs to demanding overhangs, suitable for all skill levels. The magical atmosphere of the forest, with centuries-old fir and beech trees, adds a special charm to the climbing experience.

    The best time to visit Bagni di Masino is during the spring and autumn, when the cool temperatures and dry conditions provide ideal friction on the granite rock. During these months, the valley comes alive with vibrant colors, creating a perfect environment for climbing and exploration. The proximity to San Martino offers easy access to services and accommodations, ensuring a comfortable and memorable stay.
