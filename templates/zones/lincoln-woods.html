    Bouldering in Lincoln Woods

    Lincoln Woods is a bouldering paradise in Rhode Island, offering high-quality granite with excellent friction and a variety of climbing styles. The glacially deposited boulders feature everything from technical slabs to powerful overhangs, making it a prime location for climbers of all levels.

    The best time to visit is fall through spring when the crisp temperatures provide optimal friction. Summer can be humid, but shaded areas near Olney Pond offer some relief. With parking close to the main sectors and a strong local climbing community, Lincoln Woods is a social hub as much as a climbing destination. Nearby Providence provides all necessary amenities, and the park itself offers hiking trails, picnic spots, and water activities for rest days.
