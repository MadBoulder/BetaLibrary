    Bouldering in Valcorchero

    Valcorchero, in Spain, stands out for its unique granite rock. Located in Extremadura, this area offers a wide range of bouldering problems on distinctive rock formations. The best season is winter and spring. Valcorchero is appreciated for its uniqueness and is a popular destination among climbers and boulderers.
