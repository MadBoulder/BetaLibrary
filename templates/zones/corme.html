    Bouldering in Corme

    Corme, located in Galicia, is celebrated for its granite boulders that provide a mix of crimps and slopers for boulderers. The area is famous for its picturesque coastal backdrop and offers climbing opportunities year-round, with spring and summer being the ideal seasons.
