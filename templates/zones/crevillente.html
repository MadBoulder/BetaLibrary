    Bouldering in Crevillente

    Crevillente, located in the province of Alicante in the Valencian Community, is a hidden gem for bouldering enthusiasts. Known for its stunning sandstone rocks, this area offers a variety of captivating routes that cater to climbers of all levels. The unique charm of Crevillente lies in its secluded location, providing a tranquil and unspoiled climbing environment. The Serra de Crevillent mountain range provides a picturesque backdrop for climbing, adding to the area's appeal.

    The ideal season for bouldering in Crevillente is winter, thanks to the mild weather conditions that make climbing comfortable and enjoyable. The region's landscape, characterized by dry terrain and Mediterranean vegetation such as carob, almond, and olive trees, enhances the natural beauty of the climbing experience. Crevillente remains a hidden treasure, attracting climbers looking for both challenge and serenity
