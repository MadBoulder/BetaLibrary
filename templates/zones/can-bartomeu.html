    Bouldering in Can Bartomeu

    Located on the coast of Barcelona, Can Bartomeu is a bouldering paradise in the midst of nature. It offers granite rock and is known for its stunning boulders and a variety of problems. The most popular season is winter, when the temperatures are perfect for climbing.
