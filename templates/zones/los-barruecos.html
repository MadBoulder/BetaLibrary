    Bouldering in Los Barruecos

    Los Barruecos, in Extremadura, is a unique bouldering destination. Here, massive granite blocks emerge amidst the landscape. This place is known for its wild character and isolation, providing climbers with an unforgettable experience. The ideal season is spring and autumn when the weather is pleasant.
