    Bouldering in Las tuerces

    Las tuerces, located in the province of Palencia in northern Spain, is a stunning natural labyrinth of limestone formations offering a unique bouldering experience. This area is known for its surreal karst landscape, with large rock formations that resemble sculptures, creating an otherworldly setting for climbers. The bouldering problems in Las tuerces are primarily on solid limestone, offering a range of grades suitable for both beginners and advanced climbers.

    The variety of holds, from pockets to edges and slopers, makes for a rich climbing experience with a predominance of powerful overhanging routes. The best time to visit Las tuerces is during the cooler months, particularly from autumn to spring, as summer temperatures can be quite high. Easy access to the climbing spots and the quiet, serene environment add to the appeal of Las tuerces as a top bouldering destination in Spain.
