    Bouldering in Valdesangil

    Valdesangil, situated in Béjar, is renowned for its impressive granite formations that offer a diverse range of bouldering challenges. This area is highly regarded for its picturesque surroundings and features, making it a favorite among climbers. The best time to visit is during the cooler months, and its popularity stems from the quality of rock and stunning landscapes.
