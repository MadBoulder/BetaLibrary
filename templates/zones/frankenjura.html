    Frankenjura, located in Germany, is a bouldering wonderland featuring limestone crags and overhangs. Known for its intricate crimps, pockets, and technical challenges, it draws climbers from around the world. The best season is spring and fall, making it a must-visit for those seeking a thrilling climbing experience.
