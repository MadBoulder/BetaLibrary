    Bouldering in The Rock Shop

    The Rock Shop is a celebrated bouldering haven in Wyoming, lauded for its solid rock and extensive selection of problems set in a beautiful, remote area at about 8000 feet elevation. Accessibility might require a hike or a vehicle with good clearance, making it an adventure to even reach the boulders nestled in the woods. Ideal times for a visit are spring and fall, with summer also suitable due to the high altitude.

    As a relatively new climbing area, The Rock Shop is still being developed, meaning climbers may encounter few established trails and evolving holds. The boulders are spread out, inviting climbers to explore and discover the areaâ€™s challenges.
