    Bouldering in Alcañiz

    Alcañiz is a well-kept secret in the province of Teruel, Spain. This sandstone bouldering destination provides a plethora of problems, with the best season in autumn and spring. Although less known than other places, its quality and natural beauty attract climbers seeking new experiences.
