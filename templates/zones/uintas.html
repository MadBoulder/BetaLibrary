    Bouldering in Uintas

    Uintas in Utah, USA, offers a scenic bouldering experience on gneiss rock. Set in a tranquil mountain landscape, it provides a mix of technical and powerful climbing. The area is known for its serene atmosphere and is best enjoyed during the summer.
