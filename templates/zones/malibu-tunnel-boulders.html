    Bouldering in Malibu Tunnel Boulders

    Malibu Tunnel Boulders, nestled along the rugged California coastline, offers an exceptional bouldering experience on unique basalt formations. The area is renowned for its oceanfront boulders and challenging problems, attracting climbers seeking dynamic and coastal adventures. With mild winters and cooler summers, it's a year-round destination for bouldering enthusiasts.
