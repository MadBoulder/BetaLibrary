    Bouldering in Navalosa

    Navalosa, situated in the province of Ávila, is a hidden gem for bouldering enthusiasts, renowned for its extensive granite boulders and stunning natural settings. This area, located about 130 kilometers from Madrid, boasts over 1,800 problems across various sectors, making it one of the densest bouldering areas in the region. The granite in Navalosa is of high quality, providing excellent friction and a range of challenges that cater to climbers of all skill levels, from beginners to seasoned veterans.

    The best seasons to climb in Navalosa are fall and winter, when the mild weather offers optimal conditions for bouldering. The picturesque landscape, combined with the area's growing reputation among both local and international climbers, makes it a must-visit destination.
