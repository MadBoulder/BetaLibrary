    Bouldering in Little Cottonwood Canyon

    Little Cottonwood Canyon, located in Utah near Salt Lake City, is a climbing paradise renowned for its exceptional granite boulders and stunning alpine scenery. With over 500 boulder problems, including iconic spots like Secret Garden and Five Mile Boulders, climbers can find routes that range from beginner-friendly V0s to expert-level V12s and beyond.

    The best seasons to climb in Little Cottonwood Canyon are spring, summer, and fall, although the area remains accessible year-round due to its mild winter conditions. The canyon's unique high-desert climate and elevation provide cooler temperatures, ideal for climbing even during warmer months. The combination of world-class climbing, easy accessibility from Salt Lake City, and the picturesque setting of the Wasatch Range makes Little Cottonwood Canyon a must-visit destination for bouldering enthusiasts​.
