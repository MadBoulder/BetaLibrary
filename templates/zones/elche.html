    Bouldering in Elche

    The Elche bouldering area, just north of Elche, Spain, offers a series of limestone boulders across various sectors like Las Antenas, La Cueva de al Lado, and El Dorado. Developed by local climbers, this spot includes a wide range of grades that make it ideal for climbers of different skill levels. With natural limestone formations, the area provides both technical challenges and varied terrain, from vertical faces to overhangs, appealing to those who enjoy a mix of styles.
