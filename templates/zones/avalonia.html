    Bouldering in Avalonia

    The Avalonia bouldering area in Germany is renowned for its sandstone rock formations, offering over 150 problems that range in difficulty from 3 to 8A+. The climbing style in Avalonia primarily involves technical moves on high-quality sandstone, with features that include overhangs, vertical faces, and a mix of crimps, cracks, and slopers which provide a comprehensive and challenging climbing experience.

    Located near the town of Wetter, close to Dortmund, Avalonia is easily accessible and has become a popular destination for local and visiting climbers. The area benefits from a temperate climate, making it suitable for climbing throughout the year, although the best conditions are typically found in spring and autumn when the weather is mild and the rock is less likely to be slippery from moisture.
