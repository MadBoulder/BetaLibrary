    Bouldering in Penya de s'Indi

    Penya de s'Indi, located in Menorca, Spain, is a bouldering paradise known for its stunning seaside setting and limestone cliffs. Climbers are drawn to its challenging routes, featuring pockets, crimps, and overhangs. With its mild Mediterranean climate, it's a year-round destination and offers a unique coastal climbing experience.
