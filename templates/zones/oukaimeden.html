    Bouldering in Oukaimeden

    Oukaimeden, located in the High Atlas Mountains of Morocco, is quickly becoming a prominent destination for bouldering enthusiasts. The area is renowned for its breathtaking mountainous landscapes and the unique, rough texture of its sandstone boulders, which provide a wide range of problems from easy warm-ups to high-end challenges. Climbers can test their skills on crimps, edges, and slopers across various sectors, each offering distinct features and difficulties. The altitude, reaching between 2,500 to 3,000 meters, adds an extra layer of challenge, affecting stamina and breathing. Oukaimeden is not only about climbing; it’s an opportunity to immerse in the tranquil beauty of the mountains, with options for hiking and exploring local Berber culture. Accommodations range from rustic chalets to organized camping sites, making it suitable for diverse tastes and budgets.
