    Bouldering in Larraona

    Larraona is a limestone rock climbing paradise. With stunning vertical walls and numerous boulder problems, it's a popular spot for climbers seeking technical challenges and exceptional panoramic views. The ideal seasons are spring and fall, and its reputation is built on the diversity of problems and the natural beauty of the surroundings.
