    Bouldering in La Collada

    La Collada's bouldering area in Quirós unfolds as a hidden gem within the breathtaking landscapes of Asturias, Spain. Nestled amid the limestone cliffs, this bouldering haven offers a diverse array of challenges, showcasing intricate rock formations and captivating features. Climbers, drawn to La Collada for its bouldering opportunities, will encounter a mix of pockets, overhangs, and unique routes suitable for varying skill levels.
