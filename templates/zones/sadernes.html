    Bouldering in Sadernes

    Bouldering in Sadernes has been developed recently, offering an exciting new option for climbing enthusiasts in this region. The main sector, known as Pont d'en Bleau, is located just after the Pont de Sant Valentí. It is essential to park in the designated parking areas, and from there, a 20-minute approach leads to the boulders.

    The area features some large limestone blocks, each with various climbing problems. Many of the more difficult problems have yet to be opened, the area has significant potential for new routes. Additionally, the nearby river provides an excellent opportunity to cool off during the warmer months, making this area an attractive destination for both climbing and enjoying the surrounding nature.

    The development of this bouldering area complements the already well-known sport climbing and hiking routes in Sadernes, adding another dimension to the outdoor activities available in this beautiful region of Catalonia. The combination of climbing challenges and the natural beauty of the surroundings ensures that Pont d'en Bleau will become a popular destination for climbers.
