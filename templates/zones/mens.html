    Bouldering in Mens

    Mens, located in the coast of Galicia, is known for its mesmerizing granite boulders. This bouldering haven offers a wide range of climbing challenges, from technical slabs to powerful overhangs. It's a favorite among climbers, and the best time to visit is during the cooler months for optimal grip.
