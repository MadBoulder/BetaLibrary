    Bouldering in Rifugio Barbara

    Rifugio Barbara is a bouldering paradise in Italy, renowned for its granite rock that provides a wide range of challenging problems and routes. Located in a picturesque setting in the Alps, this spot is perfect for climbers of all levels. The optimal season is spring and summer, attracting climbers from around the world.
