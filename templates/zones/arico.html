    Bouldering in Arico

    In the heart of Tenerife, the Arico area stands out as a bouldering paradise, boasting over 240 boulders - the highest concentration on the island. This bouldering haven is best enjoyed during the cooler and cloudier months, from October to April, when the open gorge offers ideal climbing conditions. Despite its popularity and extensive array of boulders, there remains potential for cleaning and first ascents, making it an ever-evolving climbing landscape. The area is predominantly child-friendly, welcoming climbers of all ages to explore its diverse routes.
