    Area overview

    La Jonquera boasts an impressive grenite rock landscape, providing boulderers with unique features like crimps, cracks, and interesting shapes. This area is favored for its challenging routes and picturesque setting, drawing climbers seeking a variety of difficulties. The best season to visit is during the cooler months to enjoy the optimal climbing conditions.
