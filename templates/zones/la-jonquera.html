    Bouldering in La Jonquera

    La Jonquera, located in the Alt Empordà region of Catalonia, is a prominent destination for bouldering, known for its impressive granite rock landscape. This place offers a variety of unique features, such as crimps, cracks, and slopers, challenging climbers of all levels. The diversity of problems, ranging from easy to extremely difficult, attracts a wide range of climbers seeking new challenges in a picturesque setting. The best time to boulder in La Jonquera is during the cooler months when climbing conditions are optimal.

    This area is not only popular for its challenging routes but also for its natural and tranquil environment, away from the hustle and bustle of the city. With more than 1,000 bouldering problems spread across various sectors, La Jonquera stands out as an unmissable destination for bouldering enthusiasts looking for a unique and rewarding experience in Catalonia.
