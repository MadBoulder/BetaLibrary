    Bouldering in Lles de Cerdanya

    Lles de Cerdanya is a paradise in the Catalan Pyrenees. It features granite boulders surrounded by lush forests. Its serene environment and diverse problems make it appealing to climbers of all levels. Spring and summer are the most popular seasons for climbing.
