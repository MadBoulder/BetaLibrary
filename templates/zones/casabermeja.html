    Bouldering in Casabermeja

    Casabermeja, located in the beautiful region of Andalusia, is renowned for its limestone rock that offers a diverse bouldering experience. Known for its pocketed terrain, climbers flock to Casabermeja to test their skills. The area's mild winters make it a year-round destination, and its reputation for high-quality problems and stunning surroundings keeps boulderers coming back.
