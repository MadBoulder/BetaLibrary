    Bouldering in Holy Boulders

    The Holy Boulders bouldering area, nestled in the Shawnee National Forest in Southern Illinois, is a top destination for climbers. Known for its high-quality sandstone boulders, it offers a wide variety of problems suitable for all skill levels. The area's stunning forested surroundings and unique rock formations create a serene and inspiring outdoor experience. The best time to climb here is during the fall and spring, when the weather is cool and the conditions are ideal for bouldering.

    Committed to conservation and sustainable use, the Holy Boulders maintains its natural beauty and ecological integrity. Climbing access is regulated to minimize environmental impact, and guidelines protect the site's historical and cultural heritage. The area also hosts community events and competitions, promoting responsible climbing practices and fostering a vibrant climbing community.
