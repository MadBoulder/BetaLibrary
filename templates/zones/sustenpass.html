    Bouldering in Sustenpass

    Nestled in the heart of the Swiss Alps, Sustenpass boasts stunning granite boulders that challenge climbers of all levels. Known for its crisp alpine air and breathtaking views, it's a favorite among enthusiasts. The best time to visit is during summer, when the cool climate makes for ideal conditions.
