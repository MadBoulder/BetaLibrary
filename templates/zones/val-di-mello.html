    Bouldering in Val di Mello

    al di Mello, in Italy, is renowned for its stunning granite. Located in an alpine valley, this climbing paradise offers a wide range of bouldering problems in a spectacular natural setting. The best time to visit is spring and summer. Val di Mello is an iconic destination for climbers and boulderers worldwide due to its beauty and the quality of its routes.
