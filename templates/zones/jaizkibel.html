    Bouldering in Jaizkibel

    Jaizkibel, in the Basque Country, is a unique bouldering area with views of the Cantabrian Sea. The sandstone rock provides a physical climbing experience and is most enjoyable during spring and summer. Its natural beauty and thrilling problems attract climbers from around the world.
