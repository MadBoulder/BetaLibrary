    Bouldering in Ghisallo

    Ghisallo, nestled in the hills above Finale Ligure, is a limestone bouldering paradise offering a diverse mix of technical sequences, overhangs, and dynamic moves. The rock’s variety—ranging from sharp crimps to smooth slopers—ensures an engaging experience for all climbers. While there are problems across all difficulty levels, the area is best suited for those pushing into the higher grades. The sectors are spread out, providing a sense of solitude despite being close to the lively coastal town of Finale Ligure.

    The best season to climb here is from autumn to spring, as the Mediterranean climate keeps conditions mild while avoiding the summer heat. The approach is straightforward, with parking spots marked on climbing platforms, and the proximity to Finale Ligure means easy access to accommodations, restaurants, and beaches.
