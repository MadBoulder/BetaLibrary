    Bouldering in La Dehesa de Candelario

    Nestled in the Candelario mountains, La Dehesa is a granite paradise with spectacular boulders. Its reputation lies in its unique and challenging formations. The best season for climbing is spring, and its popularity is attributed to its natural beauty.
