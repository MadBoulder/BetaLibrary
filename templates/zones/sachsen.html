    Located in Germany, Sachsen is famous for its gneiss rock and challenging climbing terrain. This bouldering area provides a diverse range of holds, including slopers, crimps, and jugs. Sachsen is known for its year-round climbing opportunities and its reputation as a hub for technical and powerful bouldering.
