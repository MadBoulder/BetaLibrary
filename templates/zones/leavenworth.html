    Bouldering in Leavenworth

    Leavenworth, in the United States, is renowned for its high-quality granite and stunning alpine surroundings. Climbers flock here to enjoy a wide range of boulder problems, from roofs to slabs. The preferred season is spring and summer, and its popularity is attributed to the diversity of routes and the beauty of the North Cascades of Washington.
