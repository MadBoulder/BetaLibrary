    Bouldering in Leavenworth

    Leavenworth, nestled in the heart of the North Cascades in Central Washington, is a bouldering haven known for its pristine granite boulders and picturesque alpine setting. This renowned climbing destination features over 1,200 boulder problems, ranging from V0 to V13, offering a broad spectrum of challenges from technical slabs to powerful overhangs. The stunning surroundings of Icicle Canyon and Tumwater Canyon add to the allure, providing climbers with breathtaking views as they tackle diverse routes​.

    Spring and summer are the ideal seasons for bouldering in Leavenworth, though the area is accessible year-round, with winter offering a unique climbing experience amidst occasional snow. The climbing community in Leavenworth has grown significantly, transforming it from a local favorite to a world-class bouldering destination. The combination of high-quality granite, the variety of boulder problems, and the scenic beauty of the North Cascades makes Leavenworth a must-visit for bouldering enthusiasts from around the globe​.
