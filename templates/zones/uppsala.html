    Bouldering in Uppsala

    Uppsala, in Sweden, is characterized by its distinctive gneiss rock. This area is known for its variety of bouldering routes and easy accessibility. The optimal season to visit Uppsala is spring, and its reputation is due to the blend of natural beauty and gneiss climbing opportunities.
