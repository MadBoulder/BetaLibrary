    Bouldering in Biota

    Biota, in Aragón, Spain, is a bouldering area known for its fine-grained sandstone blocks, offering a variety of climbing styles. While the rock quality varies, some sectors provide excellent problems. Its arid climate and sparse vegetation make it an ideal winter destination, though it remains relatively quiet and underused. There are seven sectors around the village, accessible via dirt tracks that are generally in good condition, except after rain.
