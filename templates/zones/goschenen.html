    Bouldering in Göschenen

    Göschenen, situated in the heart of Switzerland, is known for its metamorphic gneiss rock. The area offers diverse climbing features, from sharp crimps to comfortable jugs. It is most popular during the summer months when climbers can enjoy the picturesque Swiss landscape while bouldering.
