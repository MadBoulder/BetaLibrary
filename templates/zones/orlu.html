    Bouldering in Orlu

    Orlu, located in the heart of the Pyrenees, is a renowned bouldering paradise known for its impressive granite rock formations. This area boasts a variety of climbing features, including cracks, crimps, and slopers. The best season to visit is during late spring and early summer, making it a popular choice for those seeking an alpine bouldering adventure.
