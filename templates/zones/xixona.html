    Bouldering in Xixona

    Jijona, also known as Xixona, is a renowned bouldering destination in Spain, celebrated for its high-quality limestone formations. Located in the province of Alicante in the Valencian Community, this area offers a diverse range of bouldering problems suitable for climbers of all levels.

    Jijona is ideal for year-round climbing, but the cooler months of autumn and spring provide the most comfortable conditions. The region's mild Mediterranean climate ensures that the rock is in excellent condition, offering optimal friction for climbers. The stunning natural landscapes surrounding the bouldering areas add to the experience, making Jijona not just a place for challenging climbs but also for appreciating beautiful scenery.
