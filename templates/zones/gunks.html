    Bouldering in Gunks

    Gunks bouldering, short for Shawangunks, is a premier bouldering destination in New York State, offering a vast array of boulder problems across its principal sectors: Near Trapps, Trapps, and Peter's Kill. Known for its quartz conglomerate rock, climbers find challenges and opportunities in its varied terrain, from horizontal cracks to overhangs. This area is a haven for bouldering enthusiasts seeking quality climbs in a scenic setting.
