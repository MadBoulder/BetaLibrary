    Bouldering in Rocacorba

    Rocacorba is a climbing gem in Spain, known for its limestone rock and a wide variety of problems. Located in the Girona region, it's popular year-round, with optimal conditions in spring and autumn. Its reputation is built on its beautiful surroundings and diversity of routes.
