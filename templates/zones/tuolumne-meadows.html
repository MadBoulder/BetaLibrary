    Bouldering in Tuolumne Meadows

    Tuolumne Meadows, nestled in Yosemite National Park, is a bouldering paradise featuring beautiful granite boulders. Its reputation is built on the stunning high-country setting, where climbers can enjoy a mix of technical and powerful climbing. The best season to visit is during the summer.
