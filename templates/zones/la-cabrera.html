    Nestled in the heart of Spain, La Cabrera boasts a spectacular landscape of rugged granite boulders. Its reputation as a bouldering paradise is well-deserved, with a wide range of problems for climbers of all levels. The best season to visit is autumn, offering crisp conditions.
