    Area overview

    Laurenzoboulderfels, located in Alsace, is a gneiss paradise for climbing enthusiasts. Its boulder problems offer a variety of challenging routes in a beautiful natural setting. The optimal season is spring and summer, and its reputation is built on the quality of the rock and the diversity of problems.
