    Bouldering in Fionnay

    Fionnay, nestled in the Swiss Alps, is a boulderer's dream with its stunning granite boulders. The alpine beauty and crisp air create a remarkable climbing experience. Fionnay is most popular in the summer months, making it an ideal destination for alpine bouldering.
