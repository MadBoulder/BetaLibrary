    Bouldering in Moe's Valley

    Moe's Valley, nestled in the picturesque landscapes of Southern Utah, is a renowned bouldering destination located just minutes from St. George. This hidden gem offers climbers a unique experience with its beautifully sculpted sandstone boulders, presenting an array of challenges from V0 to V14. The area is particularly known for its grainy, orange-brown sandstone, which can be tough on the skin but provides excellent grip and a variety of holds, including slopers, crimps, and cracks.

    The prime season for climbing in Moe's Valley is from fall to spring when the cooler temperatures create optimal conditions for bouldering. The stunning desert scenery, combined with a vibrant climbing community, makes it a favorite spot for both local and visiting climbers. Over the years, Moe's Valley has grown in popularity, offering nearly 400 boulder problems in areas like Super Mario Land. Its accessibility and the surrounding beauty of the Utah desert make it a must-visit destination for bouldering enthusiasts​​.
