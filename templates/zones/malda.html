    Bouldering in Maldà

    Maldà, located in the beautiful Catalonia region of Spain, is a small bouldering paradise known for its sandstone cube. Climbers are drawn to Maldà for its stunning landscape, featuring a variety of boulders and scenic views. With a reputation for challenging problems, Maldà is popular among those seeking a physical climbing experience. The best season to visit is spring, with pleasant weather and ideal conditions.
