    Bouldering in Bowderstone

    Bowderstone, nestled in the Lake District of England, is famous for its gigantic rock boulder, Bowder Stone. This natural monolith provides diverse climbing opportunities, including crack climbs and highball bouldering. The area is popular year-round, but summer offers the best conditions.
