    Bouldering in Tintorale

    Located in Abruzzo, Italy, Tintorale has become a notable bouldering spot, greatly developed by Elias Iagnemma. Among his achievements is Ganesh, rated at 8C+, potentially the hardest boulder problem in Italy. Climbing in Tintorale demands unparalleled respect for the rock, especially considering its delicacy. Climbers must choose the right conditions to avoid damage, waiting for the rock to be completely dry after rain or high humidity, as the holds can break. This requirement underscores the area's unique climbing ethics and the stunning backdrop of Abruzzo.
