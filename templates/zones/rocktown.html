    Bouldering in Rocktown

    Located in Georgia, USA, Rocktown boasts an abundance of high-quality sandstone boulders. Climbers flock here for its unique sandstone features, including pockets, crimps, and challenging mantles. Fall and spring are ideal for bouldering, and Rocktown's reputation is solidified by its classic problems and a friendly climbing community.
