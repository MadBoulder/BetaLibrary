    Bouldering in El Tranqueru

    Nestled in the pristine landscapes of Asturias, El Tranqueru is known for its stunning limestone formations and challenging bouldering routes. The best conditions can be found in spring and autumn, making it a popular choice for climbers seeking a mix of physical and technical climbs.
