    Bouldering in Ailefroide

    Ailefroide is an alpine paradise in the French High Alps, renowned for its formidable granite boulders. The area is famous for its variety of mostly highball boulder problems and its reputation as a favorite destination for climbers. The best time to visit is in summer when conditions are ideal.
