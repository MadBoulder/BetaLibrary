    Bouldering in Wassen

    Wassen, situated in Switzerland, is renowned for its gneiss boulders that provide an array of bouldering opportunities. This area's reputation is based on its mix of technical and physical routes, making it a go-to destination for climbers. With a temperate climate, Wassen attracts boulderers year-round.
