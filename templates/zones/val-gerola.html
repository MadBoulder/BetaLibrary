    Bouldering in Val Gerola

    Val Gerola, in Italy, is a burgeoning bouldering hotspot within the Orobie Valtellinesi Natural Park. Famous for its unique reddish Verrucano Lombardo conglomerate boulders, it offers a rich variety of problems, appealing to climbers of all levels. With an altitude over 1000 meters, the area boasts year-round climbing, enhanced by safe landings thanks to local efforts.

    Val Gerola stands out for its tranquility and family-friendly environment, rivaling the nearby renowned Masino Valley. From the easily accessible boulders near Gerola Alta village to the shaded problems in the Vallise sector, climbers can enjoy over 100 problems across diverse terrains. The commitment of local climbers has seen this area gain popularity and host the inaugural GerolaSass event, signaling a bright future for bouldering in Val Gerola.
