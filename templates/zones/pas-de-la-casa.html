    Bouldering in Pas de la Casa

    Pas de la Casa, nestled in the Pyrenees, is renowned for its chaos of granite boulders. This bouldering area offers a unique experience with a variety of features, including crimps and edges. Climbers flock to Pas de la Casa for its stunning alpine landscapes and challenging problems. The best season to explore this area is during late spring and early summer.
