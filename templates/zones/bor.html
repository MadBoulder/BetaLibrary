    Bouldering in Bor

    Bor, located in the Czech Republic, is a hidden gem for boulderers. With its unique sandstone formations, it offers a variety of bouldering challenges, from delicate slabs to powerful overhangs. Bor is renowned for its peaceful setting and cool climate, making it a favorite destination during the spring and fall.
