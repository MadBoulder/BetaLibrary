    Bouldering in Hoyamoros

    Hoyamoros, located in the Sierra de Gredos in Spain, is an exceptional alpine bouldering destination renowned for its striking granite boulders and world-class problems. Situated at an altitude of 2,200 meters, Hoyamoros offers climbers a unique combination of technical and physical challenges amidst breathtaking natural scenery. The granite provides excellent friction, making it ideal for a variety of climbing styles, including crimps, slabs, and overhangs. With over 1,000 boulder problems ranging from moderate to extremely difficult, climbers of all levels can find routes that match their skills and push their limits.

    The best time to visit Hoyamoros is from late spring to autumn, as the high altitude keeps temperatures 5-10 degrees lower than the valley, providing optimal climbing conditions. The area’s serene and scenic environment, characterized by green meadows surrounded by mountains, enhances the climbing experience, offering a peaceful escape into nature. Access to Hoyamoros requires a 1.5-2 hour uphill hike from the parking area, making it a true adventure for those seeking a world-class bouldering paradise in a remote and stunning location​.
