    Bouldering in Villanueva del Rosario

    Villanueva del Rosario, nestled in Spain, is famous for its limestone boulders that present climbers with technical challenges. The area's reputation is built on its unique climbing features and the rugged beauty of the surrounding countryside. Best visited during the cooler months, Villanueva del Rosario is a hidden gem for bouldering enthusiasts.
