    Bouldering in Las Jaras

    Las Jaras is a small bouldering area situated by the reservoir near Córdoba, offering climbers the chance to engage with boulders against a picturesque forested backdrop. Beyond climbing, Las Jaras invites outdoor enthusiasts to enjoy water activities and the natural tranquility of the area, making it a perfect spot for a comprehensive outdoor adventure.
