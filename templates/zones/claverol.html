    Bouldering in Claverol

    Claverol, nestled in Catalonia, Spain, is known for its conglomerate rock, offering unique climbing opportunities. With its reputation for both technical and physical challenges, Claverol attracts climbers seeking a mix of pockets, slopers, and crimps. The ideal season to explore this area is during the cooler months.
