    Bouldering in Castle Rock State Park

    Castle Rock State Park, located in California, is renowned for its stunning sandstone formations and iconic bouldering problems. Climbers are drawn to its unique features, including cracks, crimps, and slopers. The best time to visit is during the spring and fall when the weather is ideal.
