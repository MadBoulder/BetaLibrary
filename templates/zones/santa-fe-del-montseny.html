    Bouldering in Santa Fe del Montseny

    Santa Fe del Montseny, located in the heart of Montseny Natural Park, offers bouldering enthusiasts the chance to climb on the unique granitic rocks of the area. Renowned for its stunning forested setting, Santa Fe del Montseny is a popular spot year-round, with summer providing cooler conditions. The bouldering here features a mix of technical and powerful problems.
