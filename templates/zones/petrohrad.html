    Bouldering in Petrohrad

    Petrohrad, nestled in the Czech Republic, boasts a unique blend of granite rock formations and lush forest surroundings. Its renowned granite features create a diverse climbing experience, including crimps, edges, and beautiful cracks. Climbers visit Petrohrad to test their skills on this natural wonder, which is best explored during the spring and summer.
