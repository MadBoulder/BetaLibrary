    Bouldering in Joe's Valley

    Joe's Valley in Utah is a premier bouldering destination known for its high-quality sandstone and wide range of problems suitable for all skill levels. Located a few hours southeast of Salt Lake City, the area is divided into three main sectors: Right Fork, Left Fork, and New Joe's, each offering distinctive climbing experiences from V0 to V16. This bouldering area is famed for its challenging slopers and mantles.

    The area has grown in popularity over the years, attracting climbers from all over the world, thanks to its high concentration of quality boulders and the beauty of its natural surroundings. Additionally, the local community and visiting climbers often engage in efforts to maintain and preserve the climbing environment, emphasizing responsible practices and sustainability.
