    Bouldering in Calderona

    Located in the picturesque Serra Calderona Natural Park, this bouldering area in Spain is renowned for its challenging limestone boulders. With a reputation for its technical climbs and stunning views, Calderona is a year-round destination. Climbers are drawn to its diverse problems and unique landscape.
