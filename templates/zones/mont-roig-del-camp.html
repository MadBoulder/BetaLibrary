    Bouldering in Mont-roig del Camp

    Mont-roig del Camp, situated in Tarragona, Catalunya, is a premier bouldering destination, often referred to as the Albarracín of Catalunya due to its distinctive red sandstone overhangs. This area offers a physically demanding climbing style with numerous roof-like problems, pockets, and technical mantles, making it ideal for climbers seeking challenging problems. The optimal climbing season is during the winter months when the conditions are most favorable​.

    Mont-roig del Camp is still under active development, with new routes being continuously established. This ongoing expansion promises even more diverse and challenging problems for climbers. Additionally, a new update of the bouldering guide is expected soon, providing the latest information and routes to explore. The area's unique red sandstone formations and the rigorous climbing opportunities they present make Mont-roig del Camp a must-visit destination for bouldering enthusiasts.
