    Bouldering in Selvapiana

    Selvapiana, nestled in the picturesque Valtellina region of Italy, boasts gneiss rock formations that offer a diverse bouldering experience. With its stunning natural beauty and challenging problems, it's a hidden gem for climbers. Best visited during the cooler seasons, Selvapiana is favored for its tranquility and high-quality bouldering.
