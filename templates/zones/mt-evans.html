    Bouldering in Mt Evans

    The Mt. Evans bouldering area, now known as Mt. Blue Sky, has earned acclaim for its top-tier bouldering experiences. Scattered across Colorado's accessible 14-er, these world-class boulder feature stellar granite with unique textures and incut edges. However, the approach to these boulders is challenging, and their high-altitude location limits the climbing season due to sudden storms, wind, and snow.
