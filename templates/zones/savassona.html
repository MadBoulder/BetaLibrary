    Bouldering in Savassona

    Savassona, situated near the Pantà de Sau in Catalunya, is a premier bouldering destination offering a diverse range of problems on compact sandstone. This area, located about an hour from Barcelona, is known for its beautiful natural setting and the variety of climbing styles it provides. Climbers can enjoy both physical and technical routes, with features ranging from crimps and cracks to slopers and pockets.

    The best seasons to explore Savassona are spring and autumn, when the vibrant colors of the landscape enhance the climbing experience. However, the area is accessible year-round, with winter mornings often starting foggy but clearing up for excellent climbing conditions. The tranquil environment, combined with the challenging and varied bouldering problems, makes Savassona a must-visit spot for climbers in Catalunya.
