    Area overview

    Savassona, nestled by the Pantà de Sau, offers bouldering enthusiasts a diverse range of problems on compact sandstone. The area provides a mix of physical and technical climbing styles. Be mindful of foggy mornings in cold winters, and enjoy the vibrant colors during spring and autumn.
