    Bouldering in Xistral

    Xistral, located in Galicia, is renowned for its distinctive granite rock formations that provide a unique bouldering experience. This area's reputation is built on the blend of challenging routes and the serene beauty of the Galician landscape. Xistral is most enjoyable during the cooler seasons, making it a captivating spot for boulderers.
