    Bouldering in Kerlouan

    Kerlouan, located on the scenic coast of Brittany, France, offers a stunning bouldering experience with its unique granite blocks scattered along the shoreline. The area is known for its breathtaking views of the Atlantic Ocean and offers a wide range of problems suitable for both beginners and experienced climbers. The proximity to the beach makes it an ideal destination for a mix of climbing and relaxation. While the granite here is of excellent quality, the exposure to the sea adds a challenging element to the conditions.

    With hundreds of boulders spread across various sectors, Kerlouan is best visited during spring and summer, although it can be windy. This area is famous not only for its technical climbs but also for its striking natural beauty. Climbers will enjoy the peaceful, remote environment, as well as the chance to explore hidden coves and paths along the coast.
