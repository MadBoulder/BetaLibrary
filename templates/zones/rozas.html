    Bouldering in Rozas

    Rozas, in Burgos, is known for its sandstone rock, providing numerous bouldering problems for climbers. The remote location and natural surroundings make it an ideal spot for those seeking a peaceful climbing experience. The best time to visit is during spring and autumn.
