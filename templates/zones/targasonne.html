    Bouldering in Targasonne

    Targassonne, located in the French Pyrenees, is renowned for its high-quality granite boulders. Its proximity to beautiful forests and pristine nature makes it a popular spot for climbers and boulderers. The best season to visit is spring and autumn, and it's known for its challenging yet rewarding problems.
