    Bouldering in Zarzalejo

    Zarzalejo, located about 50 kilometers northwest of Madrid, is celebrated for its beautiful granite boulders and unique climbing problems. The area boasts over 300 bouldering problems spread across various sectors, offering a range of difficulties suitable for climbers of all levels. The granite in Zarzalejo is notably abrasive, providing excellent friction and enhancing the climbing experience. Climbers can enjoy a mix of slopers, crimps, and technical moves, making it a versatile spot for year-round climbing. The landscape is characterized by open meadows and some shaded areas, providing a picturesque backdrop for your climbing sessions.

    One of the standout features of Zarzalejo is its accessibility and variety. The sectors are conveniently located near the town, making it easy to transition between different problems. The area's scenic beauty, combined with its technical challenges, attracts both local and visiting climbers. The best seasons to climb are autumn, winter, and spring, with winter being particularly favorable due to the quick-drying conditions in the open meadow setting.
