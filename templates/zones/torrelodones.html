    Bouldering in Torrelodones

    Torrelodones, located just outside Madrid, is a prime bouldering destination known for its accessible and diverse granite problems. The granite is notably abrasive, providing excellent friction and enhancing the climbing experience. Climbers can enjoy a mix of slopers, crimps, and technical moves, making Torrelodones a versatile spot for year-round climbing. The scenic beauty, combined with the proximity to the town's sports facilities, makes it an ideal location for quick and accessible bouldering sessions​.

    One of the standout features of Torrelodones is its convenient location and variety of bouldering problems. The area’s mild winters and cool summers ensure it remains a popular destination throughout the year. The generally safe landings and the picturesque backdrop of open meadows and shaded areas enhance the overall climbing experience, making Torrelodones a must-visit spot for any bouldering enthusiast.
