    Bouldering in Alcolea del Pinar

    Alcolea del Pinar, located in the province of Guadalajara, Spain, offers quality sandstone bouldering just a short drive from the city. Though a smaller area, its variety of fun and rewarding climbs, ranging up to 6 meters, makes it a popular spot for local climbers. The boulders feature overhangs, edges, and slopers, providing challenges for all skill levels. Due to the soft nature of the rock, climbing after rain is discouraged. Its proximity to nature and easy access make it a great weekend destination.
