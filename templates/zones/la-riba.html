    Area overview

    La Riba is known for its limestone boulders, providing a mix of pockets and overhangs, offering a physical and challenging climbing experience. Its eroded limestone presents various routes mainly in the mid-grades, attracting climbers looking for diverse challenges. It’s most popular in the warmer months, inviting climbers to enjoy the boulders and the nearby river.
