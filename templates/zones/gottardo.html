    Bouldering in Gottardo

    Gottardo is a high-altitude bouldering paradise nestled in the Swiss Alps near the Gotthard Pass. Sitting at around 2000 meters, this area is only accessible from late spring to early autumn when the snow clears. The striking granite boulders provide a mix of technical and powerful climbing, with delicate slopers, crimps, and compression moves. The 24 sectors boast over 800 problems, catering to all levels, from 5a to 8c+. Its remote setting and constant discovery of new lines make it a true gem for those who enjoy both exploration and hard climbing.

    The alpine environment offers breathtaking views, and the crisp mountain air enhances friction, making summer and early autumn the prime seasons. Due to the vast spread of the boulders, navigation can be challenging, adding to the adventure. Unlike more commercialized Swiss bouldering destinations, Gottardo remains an "unsung" spot, attracting dedicated climbers in search of a raw and rewarding experience. Whether tackling steep overhangs or intricate slabs, this area delivers a unique blend of challenge and natural beauty.
