    Bouldering in La Capelle

    La Capelle, nestled in the French countryside, is a bouldering haven with its unique sandstone rock formations. Climbers are drawn to this area for its challenging routes and abundant pockets and crimps. Spring and autumn are the best seasons to visit, offering pleasant temperatures and good friction.
