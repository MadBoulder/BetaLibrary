    Bouldering in Cabo Negro

    Cabo Negro, situated in Spain, is a hidden gem for bouldering enthusiasts. Its picturesque limestone formations over the sea offer a variety of holds, including pockets and edges. Climbing conditions are best during the cooler months, making it a preferred spot for those seeking tranquility and technical challenges.
