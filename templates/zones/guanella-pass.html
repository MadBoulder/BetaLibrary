    Bouldering in Guanella Pass

    Nestled south of Georgetown in Colorado, Guanella Pass has become a climbing haven with its diverse crags and sub-alpine boulders. While some crags on the Grant side may lack appeal for bouldering, recent years have seen the emergence of high-quality boulder problems. Accessible for most cars, though 4-wheel drive is advised for the pass, Guanella Pass offers a mix of climbing opportunities. Despite occasional closures, many boulders remain accessible, contributing to its allure.
