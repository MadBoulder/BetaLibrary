    Bouldering in Buñol

    The Buñol bouldering area, located near Valencia, Spain, is a key destination for climbers. It offers over 500 bouldering problems spread across a variety of sectors, set against a rugged, natural backdrop. The area's limestone boulders provide diverse climbing challenges, from technical slabs to steep overhangs, making it a versatile spot for climbers of all levels. The recently published guidebook on Buñol’s climbing zones provides detailed information, including high-resolution photos, maps, and access details, ensuring visitors can explore with ease.
