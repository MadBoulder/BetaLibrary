    Bouldering in El Regalito

    El Regalito, located in Lanzarote, is a hidden gem for boulderers. Its unique volcanic rock formations offer challenging climbs with a rugged landscape. This secluded spot is known for its year-round climbing conditions and is a popular destination for those seeking adventure in the Canary Islands.
