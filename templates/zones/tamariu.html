    Bouldering in Tamariu

    The Tamariu bouldering area in Catalunya is a delightful spot for climbers, featuring hard granite boulders with various problems that cater primarily to lower height and easier climbs. The boulders are spread along the picturesque Costa Brava coastline, making it an appealing destination for those who enjoy climbing with a stunning seaside backdrop. The problems are accessible and ideal for both beginners and those looking for a relaxed climbing session.

    One of the unique aspects of Tamariu is the option for deep water soloing, adding an adventurous twist for climbers who want to challenge themselves above the sea. However, it's important to note that the area offers no shade, with full sun exposure throughout the day. Despite this, the proximity to the sea makes it a popular summer destination, allowing climbers to cool off in the crystal-clear waters after a session on the rocks.
