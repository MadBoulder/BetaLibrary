    Bouldering in Exmoor

    Exmoor Coast Bouldering Area, featuring the sectors of Lynmouth and Hurlstone Point, is a captivating haven for adventurous boulderers. Explore the mysteries of the coastline between Combe Martin in North Devon and Minehead in Somerset. The area is characterized by its esoteric charm, offering climbs in secluded and hidden corners, creating a backwater atmosphere for those seeking a unique bouldering experience. Off the Radar climbs add an element of intrigue, inviting boulderers to explore less-charted territories along the stunning coastal stretch.
