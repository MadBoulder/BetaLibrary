    Bouldering in Joshua Tree

    Joshua Tree, in California, is a renowned bouldering destination famous for its captivating granite boulders and iconic desert landscapes. The park becomes a climber's paradise during the winter season, offering cooler temperatures that are ideal for bouldering. The unique character of Joshua Tree, combined with its classic routes and challenging problems, attracts climbers from all corners of the globe. With thousands of boulders scattered across the park, climbers can find everything from highball challenges to technical slabs, making it a must-visit destination for bouldering enthusiasts.
