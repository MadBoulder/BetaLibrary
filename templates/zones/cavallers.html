    Area overview

    Cavallers, nestled in the Pyrenees of Spain, is a bouldering paradise with granite rock that offers a different climbing experience. With a variety of features, from crimps to jugs, it's a favorite among climbers. The best season is in the summer when the weather is most accommodating. Cavallers is renowned for its bouldering challenges and beautiful mountain scenery.
