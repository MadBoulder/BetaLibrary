    Bouldering in Cavallers

    Cavallers, located in the region of Catalunya in Spain, is a premier destination for bouldering enthusiasts. This area, nestled in the stunning Pyrenees, is renowned for its high-quality granite rock, offering a unique climbing experience with a variety of features including crimps, jugs, and slopers. The diverse range of problems available makes it a favorite among climbers of all levels. The best time to visit Cavallers is during the summer when the weather conditions are most accommodating for outdoor activities.

    The bouldering sectors in Cavallers, such as those around the dam, provide an extensive array of bouldering problems that cater to different skill levels. The serene and pristine environment of the Pyrenees enhances the overall climbing experience, making Cavallers not just a spot for challenging climbs, but also a place to enjoy the natural beauty and tranquility of the mountains. This combination of technical climbing challenges and spectacular landscapes solidifies Cavallers' reputation as a top bouldering destination in Catalunya and Spain​.
