Bouldering in Egypt

Bouldering in Egypt unfolds amidst the captivating landscapes of the Sinai Peninsula, particularly in the coastal town of Dahab.

The emerging bouldering scene in Egypt, while not widely known, offers enthusiasts the chance to explore intriguing rock formations, with Wadi Qnai being a notable destination. The Red Sea cliffs provide a scenic backdrop, adding to the allure of bouldering in this region.

As the climbing community grows, Egypt's unique geological features and diverse landscapes make it an increasingly attractive destination for bouldering enthusiasts.
