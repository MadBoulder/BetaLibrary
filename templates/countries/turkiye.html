Bouldering in Türkiye

Bouldering in Turkey is an emerging scene with a diverse range of landscapes offering exciting opportunities for climbers. The country’s unique blend of rocky terrains and historical sites creates an alluring backdrop for outdoor enthusiasts. Among the standout areas is Bafa Lake, known for its coarse granite boulders set amidst olive groves and ancient ruins. Other notable destinations include the Aladağlar National Park and areas around Antalya, offering a mix of technical problems on limestone and sandstone.

Turkey’s bouldering community is steadily growing, with local and international climbers exploring both developed and untouched areas. The climate varies by region, with winter bouldering favored in warmer spots like southern Turkey and spring or autumn being ideal in central and northern areas. The combination of rugged landscapes, rich history, and favorable climbing conditions makes Turkey a promising destination for bouldering enthusiasts.
