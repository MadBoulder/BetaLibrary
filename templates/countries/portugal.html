Bouldering in Portugal

Portugal, a hidden gem for bouldering, welcomes climbers with its diverse landscapes. From the coastal boulders of Sintra to the granite formations in Geres National Park, the country offers unique and less-explored climbing destinations.

The Algarve region adds a coastal twist to the experience. Portugal's climbing community is friendly, and the local gyms contribute to the growing interest in the sport.

While not as renowned as other European spots, Portugal's bouldering scene is gaining recognition for its unexplored beauty and diverse challenges.
