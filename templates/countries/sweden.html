Bouldering in Sweden

Sweden beckons bouldering enthusiasts with its unique climbing landscape.

Kjugekull, on the east coast, surprises with over 1100 problems in a small area. In Vastervik, scattered crags around the city, including Rödaväggen and Lysingsbadet, offer diverse climbing potential.

Venture to Eskön for a taste of remote climbing or explore Uppsala near Stockholm, known for Focksta's excellent rock quality.

Gävle, further north, introduces local gems like Trodje. Göteborg reveals Hönö island's distinctive bouldering.

Embrace Sweden's unique grading system and diverse opportunities, making it an appealing destination for bouldering aficionados seeking lesser-known challenges.
