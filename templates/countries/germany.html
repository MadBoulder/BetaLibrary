Bouldering in Germany

Bouldering in Germany unfolds against a diverse backdrop, with prominent regions such as Frankenjura gaining acclaim for its world-renowned sport climbing.

The limestone crags of Frankenjura, although more famous for sport climbing, still offer a unique bouldering culture. Southwest Germany hosts bouldering gems like Kalimt in Pfalz and Felsenmeer in Odenwald, each contributing to the varied climbing scene in the country.
